Want to stay ahead of the curve on critical gRPC announcements? We’ve got you covered! Introducing grpc-io-announce, a new mailing list dedicated to delivering essential updates directly to your inbox. This high-signal channel cuts through the noise and focuses on what matters most, including: Security vulnerabilities and reliability issues: Be the first to know about security concerns and get timely updates on fixes and mitigations. Major platform support changes: Stay informed about changes in support for different platforms.
