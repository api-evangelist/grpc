Next Community Meeting: Thursday, August 31, 2017 11am Pacific Time (US and Canada) General Announcements Call for Papers: CloudNativeCon CloudNativeCon gathers all CNCF (Cloud Native Computing Foundation) projects under a single roof. Presenters will be talking about their experiences with Kubernetes, Prometheus, OpenTracing, Fluentd, Linkerd, gRPC, CoreDNS, containerd, rkt and CNI. The call for papers period will be ending on Monday, August 21, 2017.
